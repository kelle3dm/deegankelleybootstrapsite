About Deegan Marie Media

My name is Deegan Kelley and I am the one behind the camera. Most importantly, I want everyone to know that I am a servant and daughter of Jesus. I am in love with making people feel loved, cared for, and valued. Currently, I am a student at James Madison University pursuing a double major in communication studies and media arts and design.
